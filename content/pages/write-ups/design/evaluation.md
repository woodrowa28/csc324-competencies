# Evaluation


What was the design of your user study to evaluate your artifact?

> I conducted the evaluation of our initial paper prototype. We designed the prototype itself and the experimental script primarily to see how intuitive our proposed layout was.

What were the results of this user study?

> Having a tutorial may create a story/gameplay mismatch, as give players background and then essentially make them do the same introductory idle process twice. Adding sound effects and dynamic UI could help keep people engaged. Another engagement boost is accomodating different playstyles (min/maxing, exploring, casual, etc). Finally, the production mechanics seemed to match well with the story and vibe we were trying to convey.

What were the main points of future development that you took away from this activity?

> Idle games appeal to a very specific group of players. If we want our group to be enjoyed by as many people as possible, we need to keep many different playstyles and levels of experience with the genre in mind. It might be beneficial to implement more features, even if each is a little less detailed, than to sink all of our resources into making the "number go up" aspect perfect.
